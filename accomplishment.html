Musically gifted

Piano opened a doorway into music that no other instrument could, the accessibility and freedom you get with a digital keyboard let me practice and have fun making new things in ways my traditional piano couldn't and just being able to pickup those instruments at all was a huge goal and i crushed it, i know i know not a super major accomplishment but it still means so much to me to be able to connect with other through music and playing together and its been a major goal in my life to be musically tallented.
